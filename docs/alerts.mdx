Alerts help your team stay aware of new or changing opportunities without manually checking the same searches every day.

## Common alert sources

Alerts may be tied to:

- Saved searches
- Followed opportunities
- Important opportunity updates
- Administrative or team-level notification settings

## Before turning on alerts

Review the saved search quality first. If a saved search is too broad, alerts will feel noisy. Tighten the search, then enable notifications for the people who should act on the results.

## Troubleshooting

If alerts are not useful, check:

- Whether the saved search returns the expected opportunities
- Whether the user has notification settings enabled
- Whether the email address on the account is correct
- Whether the opportunity is new, updated, or already reviewed
