The **Opportunities** page is the main place to inspect government opportunities and decide what deserves more attention.

## Review a result

Open an opportunity to review:

- Title, agency, dates, and identifiers
- Summary and source details
- Documents or extracted text when available
- Insight answers generated for your organization
- Follow, bookmark, export, or pipeline actions

## Narrow the list

Use filters to reduce the list to opportunities that match your team:

- Keyword or phrase
- Agency or office
- Opportunity type
- Posted date or due date
- Place of performance
- Set-aside or eligibility signals
- Saved-search filters
- Insight answer filters

## Good operating habit

When a search view is useful more than once, save it as a **Saved Search**. That makes the criteria repeatable and gives your team a cleaner base for alerts and insight generation.
