Billing pages help administrators review plan status, payment details, and account limits.

## What admins should check

- Current plan
- Renewal or billing status
- Seat usage
- Payment method status
- Any custom plan terms that apply to the organization

## When to contact support

Contact AcornBids if:

- A signup link does not match the expected plan
- A payment method is not accepted
- Seat limits do not match your agreement
- Your organization needs a custom plan
