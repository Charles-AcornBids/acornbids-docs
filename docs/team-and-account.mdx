Use account settings to keep access, roles, and organization details current.

## Team access

Administrators should review:

- Which users belong to the organization
- Whether inactive users still need access
- Whether new users have completed signup
- Whether users are in the right organization

## Account settings

Users should keep their profile and notification details current so alerts and team handoffs go to the right place.

## Security habit

Remove or deactivate access when a teammate no longer needs AcornBids. This is especially important when your organization stores opportunity notes, pipeline decisions, or customer-specific strategy.
