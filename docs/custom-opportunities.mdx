Custom opportunities let your team track work that is not already available in the normal opportunity feed.

## When to add one

Use a custom opportunity for:

- Customer conversations that are not public yet
- Email-forwarded opportunities
- Local or niche solicitations
- Opportunities from partner portals
- Work your team wants to evaluate before a formal posting exists

## What to include

Include enough detail for the opportunity to be useful later:

- Title
- Customer or agency
- Due date or expected timing
- Source URL or source note
- Description
- Attachments or pasted source text when available

## Keep it traceable

If the opportunity came from an email, call, or external portal, include the source context in the notes. That makes later qualification and team handoff cleaner.
