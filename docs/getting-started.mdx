AcornBids helps your team find relevant government opportunities, save repeatable searches, answer qualification questions, and track promising work.

## Start with this workflow

1. Review **Opportunities** to see active matches.
2. Create a **Saved Search** for a market, agency, geography, keyword set, or service area you want to monitor.
3. Add **Questions and Answers** for the qualification criteria your team checks repeatedly.
4. Generate or review answers on the opportunities that matter.
5. Move strong fits into your **Pipeline** so follow-up work is visible.
6. Turn on **Alerts** for the searches and follows that need ongoing attention.

## Key terms

| Term | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| Opportunity | A contract, grant, award, or related record your team can evaluate. |
| Saved Search | A reusable set of filters for a market, customer, capability, or capture lane. |
| Question | A reusable prompt AcornBids can answer against opportunity data and documents. |
| Custom Opportunity | An opportunity your team adds manually when it is not already in the public feed. |
| Pipeline | A workspace for tracking opportunities your team wants to pursue or review. |
| Alert | A notification tied to saved searches, followed opportunities, or important changes. |
